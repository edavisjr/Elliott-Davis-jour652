By Elliott Davis Jr.


USVI air visitors by month, 2018 versus 2017

Hurricanes Irma and Maria dealt a blow to the U.S. Virgin Islands' tourism industry. Air visitor numbers have been down dramatically each month throughout 2018 compared to the year prior, especially during the "high season" in the winter months.

Source: U.S. Virgin Islands Bureau of Economic Research


Number of USVI hotel rooms after the hurricanes

The USVI's hotels were among the many buildings and structures severely damaged by the hurricanes. The Territory lost approximately 68% of its hotel rooms after the storms. Many of the Territory's major hotels -- including the Marriott Frenchman's Reef on St. Thomas and Caneel Bay on St. John -- will not reopen until late 2019 or 2020.

Source: U.S. Virgin Islands Bureau of Economic Research
